Responsible Gaming

⚠️ IMPORTANT: GORA BROTHERS is a free-to-play platform for entertainment purposes only. No real money is involved. Please play responsibly and within your means.

1. Our Commitment to Responsible Gaming

GORA BROTHERS is committed to promoting responsible gaming practices. We believe that gaming should be an enjoyable form of entertainment. We encourage all users to play responsibly and to seek help if they feel their gaming habits are becoming problematic.

2. Age Restrictions

GORA BROTHERS is strictly for users aged 18 years and above. We do not permit access to our platform for anyone under 18 years of age. By using our platform, you confirm that you are at least 18 years old and comply with all applicable laws and regulations in your jurisdiction.

3. Free-to-Play Model

GORA BROTHERS operates on a 100% free-to-play model. No real money is involved in any games or transactions. All games use virtual currency for entertainment purposes only. There is no possibility of winning real money, prizes, or any monetary value.

4. Setting Limits

We encourage users to set personal limits on their gaming activities:

  • Set a time limit for each gaming session
  • Take regular breaks during extended play
  • Do not use gaming as a way to escape problems or negative emotions
  • Maintain a healthy balance between gaming and other activities
  • Never neglect work, school, family, or social responsibilities

5. Signs of Problem Gaming

If you experience any of the following, you may benefit from seeking help:

  • Spending more time gaming than intended
  • Thinking about gaming constantly
  • Neglecting personal, professional, or educational responsibilities
  • Gaming to escape stress, anxiety, or depression
  • Difficulty reducing or controlling gaming time
  • Experiencing mood swings related to gaming
  • Lying to friends or family about gaming habits

6. Support Resources

If you or someone you know is struggling with gaming habits, please reach out to professional support services:

  • National Council on Problem Gambling: 1-800-522-4700 (USA)
  • Gamblers Anonymous: www.gamblersanonymous.org
  • Problem Gambling Services: Available in most countries
  • Mental Health Professionals: Seek counseling from qualified therapists
  • Support Groups: Join community support groups for shared experiences

7. Self-Exclusion

If you feel you need a break from gaming, you can contact our support team to request temporary or permanent account suspension. We will honor your request and help you take control of your gaming habits.

8. Parental Controls

Parents and guardians are encouraged to use parental control software to restrict access to gaming websites. GORA BROTHERS is not intended for minors, and we recommend that parents monitor their children's online activities.

9. Fair Play and Transparency

GORA BROTHERS uses certified Random Number Generators (RNG) to ensure fair gameplay. All games are designed to provide an authentic gaming experience while maintaining the highest standards of fairness and transparency. We do not manipulate game outcomes or engage in any unfair practices.
