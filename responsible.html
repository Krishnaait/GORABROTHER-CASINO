Responsible Gaming

üí° GORA BROTHERS is committed to promoting responsible gaming practices. Gaming should be fun and entertaining, not a source of stress or financial problems.

1. Gaming as Entertainment

GORA BROTHERS is designed for entertainment purposes only. Games should be played for fun and relaxation, not as a way to make money or solve financial problems. Remember that the outcome of all games is determined by chance.

2. Set Time Limits

We recommend setting time limits for your gaming sessions. Take regular breaks to maintain a healthy balance between gaming and other activities. Do not let gaming interfere with your work, studies, or personal relationships.

3. Set Spending Limits

Although GORA BROTHERS is completely free and involves no real money, we encourage users to be mindful of the time they spend on the platform. Excessive gaming can become a habit.

4. Never Chase Losses

If you experience a losing streak in games, do not attempt to recover losses by playing more. Accept losses as part of the game and take a break if needed.

5. Avoid Gaming Under Influence

Do not use this platform while under the influence of alcohol or drugs. Gaming requires focus and clear judgment.

6. Keep Gaming in Perspective

Remember that all games on GORA BROTHERS are based on chance. There is no strategy that guarantees winning. Outcomes are random and unpredictable.

7. Warning Signs of Problem Gaming

If you experience any of the following, please seek help:

  • Spending more time gaming than intended
  • Neglecting work, school, or personal relationships due to gaming
  • Feeling anxious or irritable when not gaming
  • Using gaming as an escape from problems
  • Lying to others about the amount of time spent gaming
  • Experiencing financial difficulties due to gaming

8. Seek Help if Needed

If you believe you have a problem with gaming, please reach out to appropriate resources:

  • Talk to a family member or trusted friend
  • Consult a mental health professional
  • Contact local support organizations for gaming addiction
  • Reach out to GORA BROTHERS support team

9. Parental Controls

Parents and guardians should monitor their children's internet usage and ensure they do not access GORA BROTHERS. This website is strictly for users 18 years and older.
