How to Play Blackjack

  1. Deal Cards: You and the dealer each receive 2 cards
  2. View Your Hand: Check your cards and their total value
  3. Make a Decision: Choose to HIT (get another card) or STAND (keep your current hand)
  4. Hit Strategy: Request additional cards if your total is below 17
  5. Bust Risk: If your total exceeds 21, you automatically lose
  6. Dealer Plays: The dealer must hit on 16 and stand on 17
  7. Compare Hands: Whoever is closer to 21 without busting wins
  8. Blackjack: Get 21 with your first two cards for an instant win!
